Driver License Renewal

REQUIRED
You must provide a clear photo of your current, expired driver's license as well as your passport photo.

TIMELINE
The temporary driver's license may be obtained in two days, but the original license requires forty days.

Attach Two Required Documents

  • Current Driver's License
  • Your Passport Photograph
